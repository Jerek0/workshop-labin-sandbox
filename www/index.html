How can I care for and maintain my SWAG garments?

To ensure the longevity and beauty of your SWAG garments, we recommend following these care instructions:

  • Washing: We recommend following the care label instructions on each garment. In general, it's best to hand wash your SWAG pieces in cold water with a gentle detergent. Avoid soaking for extended periods and never wring or twist the fabric. After washing, gently squeeze out excess water and lay the garment flat to dry.
  • Ironing: If necessary, use a low-temperature iron or steam to remove wrinkles. Always iron inside out or place a cloth between the iron and the garment to protect delicate details and prints.
  • Special Care: Some SWAG pieces may have specific care requirements due to delicate fabrics or intricate embellishments. Please refer to the individual product care instructions provided with your purchase for any special care considerations.
By following these care guidelines, you can help preserve the quality and beauty of your SWAG garments, ensuring that they continue to bloom and bring you joy for years to come.
